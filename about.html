OUR STORY

Passion passed down from generation to generation.

A FAMILY THING.

"The passion passed down from our dad is clear in the way we run the place."

We are a Black-owned, family-operated business located in the heart of Mid-Wilshire. What started as a dream is now a neighborhood staple. We aren't just serving coffee; we are serving energy.

Whether you are stopping by for our massive Coffee Cake, a Honey Vanilla Latte, or just to catch a vibe on our patio, we want you to feel the love. As we like to ask our customers: "Do you want to add love to the coffee?" The answer is always double.
